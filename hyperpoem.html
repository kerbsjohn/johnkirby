Love’s Guiding Light

Love for God, my heart’s true guide,
In His embrace, I stand with pride.

Love for family, warm and strong,
A bond that holds my whole life long.

Love for friends, so pure and bright,
A hand to hold in darkest night.

Love for self, a journey deep,
Through highs and lows, a soul to keep.

Love for passion, fire untamed,
A dream pursued, a heart inflamed.

Through all these loves, life shines anew,
For love is light, and love is true.
